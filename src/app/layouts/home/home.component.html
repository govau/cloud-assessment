You can pause the assessment with the Save & Exit button. To resume your assessment, simply return using the same device and browser.

About the Cloud Assessment Tool

The Cloud Assessment Tool (CAT) helps government agencies self-assess if a cloud service is suitable for a specific business context – by considering relevant compliance, commercial, operability, support and security requirements.

The CAT is not a standardised or objective assessment of a cloud vendor or service. It’s a guide to help agencies understand what qualities are important for their needs.

When you start, you’ll be asked about the cloud service and how you expect to use it. You may need to do some research to answer some questions. We’ve provided information and links to help you find relevant information quickly.

When you submit your answers, you’ll get a tailored assessment report which highlights any potential misalignment between the cloud service and your needs. You’ll also get extra information to help you understand the results and next steps.

The CAT does not ask for any personal or identifiable details. Information you provide may be used by the DTA to help us improve the CAT.
